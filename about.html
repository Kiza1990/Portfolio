About Me

My name is Kieran Halligan. I am 35 years old and currently working towards becoming a Junior Software Developer.

I recently completed an intensive coding bootcamp with CodeNation, where I developed a solid foundation in software development, particularly in web technologies and Python programming.

With a background in customer-facing roles, I bring strong communication, problem-solving, and organisational skills into my transition into tech. I've discovered a genuine passion for building engaging and functional projects — from interactive games to responsive websites.

I'm confident working with HTML, CSS, and Python, and I'm currently expanding my skillset with JavaScript. I'm a creative thinker and lifelong learner who enjoys exploring new tools, frameworks, and best practices to grow as a developer.

Outside of development, I am a proud husband to Camille and father to two amazing daughters, Clara-Rose (4) and Maeve (1). Spending time with my family is incredibly important to me — whether it's enjoying weekends outdoors going for walks or to the park, family movie nights, or supporting my girls as they grow.

In my free time, I am also passionate about football, digital creativity, and community-building. I run my own Discord server for Competitive Gaming Zone (CGZ), a community centered around PUBG Console tournaments and events, where I manage everything from scheduling to branding and engagement.

Whether I'm tackling challenges on Codewars, refining layouts in Canva, or learning a new coding concept, I approach every project with curiosity, dedication, and a drive to keep improving.
