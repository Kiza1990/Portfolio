Other Creative Work

Beyond coding, I manage and grow Competitive Gaming Zone (CGZ), a vibrant online community focused on PUBG Console events.

I design CGZ’s visual content using Canva — including tournament graphics, social media visuals, and branding materials — which has helped me develop a strong sense of layout and design, even without a formal graphic design background.

I also manage the CGZ Discord server, home to over 400 members. My role as the owner includes organizing tournaments, coordinating sign-ups, moderating discussions, and automating routine tasks to ensure smooth operations.

CGZ is a space where I combine creativity, technical skills, and community leadership — and it continues to be one of my most fulfilling projects.
